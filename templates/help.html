Lifter Kite: Definition and Purpose

What is a Lifter Kite? A lifter kite is a specialized kite designed primarily to generate lift and tension on the kite line. It's the foundational component of a kite system that enables flight.

Purpose: Generate Lift, Provide Tension, Serve as Anchor Point, Maintain Position.

What a Lifter Kite is NOT: NOT Decorative, NOT Steerable, NOT Kite Laundry, NOT the Focus of This Project.

Kite Laundry: Definition and Purpose

What is Kite Laundry? Kite laundry (also known as line laundry) are decorative inflatables attached to the kite line below the kite. They serve purely aesthetic purposes and are wind-driven.

Purpose: Visual Enhancement, Entertainment, Personal Expression, Community Tradition.

How Kite Laundry Works: Wind-Driven, Horizontal Orientation, Spinning Motion, Minimal Aerodynamic Impact.
